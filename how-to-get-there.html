How to get there?

Fly from Manila to Dumaguete via Cebu Pacific or Philippine Airlines from Monday to Friday. From Dumaguete Airport, travel by van or motorcab to Dumaguete Port. Book a ferry to Siquijor.

From Manila

1. Travel via Lite Ferry to Larena Siquijor, Wednesdays and Saturdays at 2pm.

2. Travel by bus to Liloan, Cebu and take Maayo Shipping leaving for Larena Siquijor at 2am on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays, or on a daily basis, take a fast craft to Sibulan, Negros Oriental, proceed to Dumaguete City Port then book a ferry to Siquijor.

From Cebu City

1. Travel via Lite Ferry to Larena Siquijor, Saturdays at 7pm.

2. Take Ocean Jet from Tagbilaran to Dumaguete, Mondays and Fridays only. Book a ferry to Siquijor.

From Bohol

Book a ferry to Bacolod. Catch a bus to Dumaguete. Book a ferry to Siquijor.

From Iloilo

1. From Dapitan City or Zamboanga City, take a ferry to Dumaguete City. Book a ferry to Siquijor.

2. From Plaridel, take Lite Ferry to Larena Siquijor, Sundays at 1pm.

3. From other points of Mindanao, travel to Siquijor via Cebu City.
